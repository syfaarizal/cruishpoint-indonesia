About CruisePoint Indonesia

CruisePoint Indonesia is a trusted recruitment and legal assistance agency specializing in the international cruise industry. With extensive experience in connecting Indonesian seafarers to global cruise lines, we provide comprehensive support — from preparation and document assistance to post‑contract guidance.

We assist crew members in processing essential documents such as passports, seaman books, and other related permits. For those in need of official endorsement, we also issue reference letters to support passport applications and other maritime requirements.

Our mission is to help Indonesian talents navigate the cruise career path with confidence and readiness, backed by legal knowledge and industry experience.
